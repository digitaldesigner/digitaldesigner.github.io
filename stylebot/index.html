Installation

  1. Download Stylebot for Chrome or Firefox
  2. Copy this CSS to your clipboard
  3. Visit https://secure.helpscout.net/ and open Stylebot
  4. Open Stylebot and paste your CSS into the "Code" tab (bottom)
  5. That's it!
